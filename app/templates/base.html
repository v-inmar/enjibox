Offset

Offsetting an outgoing item allows for that item to be saved and tracked but will not count towards the total perodic amount.

For example

Person A used a credit card to purchase a ticket for the price of 5.00. Person A then pays the credit card 5.00 from her debit card. Person A then saved both as seperate outgoing item. Her current total expenses is 10.00.

However, this result is not correct. In actuality, Person A only spent 5.00 in total. Person A can offset the debit card outgoing item when paying the credit card to make that item not count towards the total amount but still keeps track of when Person A used her debit card.
